Main Points

The primary person is represented by any buyer who has a minimal experience with a smartphone. This application is perfect for busy users who don't have time to search in every store the best product or offer. For some extra functionalities, the user needs to use the camera to scan a qr code. Also, if he has a social account, he can share or receive data.

Goals

Goals are the reasons users perform tasks, not the tasks.

  • Practical Goals: not necessary to go shopping
  • Personal Goals: save money
  • Business Goals: see faster the offers anywhere and anytime
Detailed Description

The application focus on buyers who want to save time and money. The buyer can select a specific location and he will receive the best offers from that specific store. He can introduce the data from application or he can scan a qr code which it will be present in a specific store and the application will be prefield with the specific offers.

The user can see all offers by specific categories and specific store. Also, he can share an offer with a facebook friend. If he likes an offer, the other users will see that the offer is liked by other people.

Frustrations and Pain Points

The user can have difficulties with scanning the qr code. Even if the user has a tutoruial at the beggining on how to use it, he may not pay so much attention and lately he will have some blurs. The application won't be able to use by blind people.

Scenarios

Ioana is at the mall and she wants to buy herself something beautiful, but she doesn't have so much money. She scan with her phone a qr code found in Palas. The main page from the application is updated with the best offers from Palas. She shoose the 'Beauty' category and the list is again updated. She found an offer from Douglas where if she buy 2, she will pay 3. That's sounds fantastic. She share this offer with her best friend on facebook and she goes to buy it
